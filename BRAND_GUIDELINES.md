# Wityliti.io Brand Guidelines

## 1. Brand Identity
**Wityliti.io** stands at the intersection of **Climate Technology** and **Cyber Security**. Our visual identity must convey trust, innovation, sustainability, and digital resilience.

- **Mission**: To rebuild the web for the AI era, fostering a sustainable and secure digital future.
- **Tone**: Professional, Futuristic, Reliable, Eco-conscious.

## 2. Logo
The logo represents the convergence of nature and code.
- **Primary Logo**: [Use `public/assets/logo.svg`]
- **Usage**:
  - Light Background: Use Dark version.
  - Dark Background: Use Light/White version.
  - Clear Space: Maintain 24px clear space around the logo.

## 3. Typography
We use a modern, sans-serif font stack to ensure readability and a tech-forward feel.
- **Primary Font**: `Inter` (or system default sans-serifs for performance).
- **Headings**: Bold, tight tracking (-0.02em).
- **Body**: Regular, legible line-height (1.5).

## 4. Color Palette
Our palette blends the "Climate" (Green/Teal) with "Cyber" (Deep Blue/Dark).

### Primary Colors
- **Climate Green**: `#10B981` (Tailwind `emerald-500`) - Used for primary actions, success states, and eco-highlights.
- **Cyber Blue**: `#0F172A` (Tailwind `slate-900`) - Used for backgrounds, text, and structure.

### Theme Variables (CSS)
| Variable | Color | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| `--primary` | `158 64% 39%` (Emerald) | Primary buttons, active states. |
| `--background` | `222 47% 11%` (Slate 900) | Main background (Dark Mode default). |
| `--foreground` | `210 40% 98%` (Slate 50) | Main text color. |
| `--accent` | `180 100% 50%` (Cyan) | Highlights, glow effects. |

## 5. Imagery & Icons
- **Icons**: Use `Lucide React` icons. Thin strokes (1.5px or 2px), consistent styling.
- **Photography/Graphics**: High-fidelity, abstract representations of data, nature, and networks. Avoid generic stock photos.

## 6. UI/UX Principles
- **Animations**: Smooth, physics-based (using Framer Motion).
- **Glassmorphism**: Subtle usage for cards and overlays.
- **Mobile First**: Simple, touch-friendly layouts.
- **Desktop Excellence**: Immersive hover effects, parallax, and rich visuals.
